There isn't a catch I can hide. The catch is on me: I'm betting a month of work on the idea that if I do it well, you'll happily pay me on day 31. If I'm wrong, I just worked for free for a month. I'm OK with that risk. I'm not OK with you paying me before I've earned it.

High patient LTV. Geographically locked demand. Independent practices being squeezed by DSOs that out-spend them on Google. Des Moines specifically is a mid-sized market with strong indie dental presence, fewer high-spend agency competitors than Austin or Miami, and a dental community that talks.
