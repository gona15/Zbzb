Privacy.

Last updated · May 17, 2026

I'm Arman. I run this site alone, from Sulaymaniyah, Kurdistan. This page is the short, honest version of what I do with the information you give me when you fill out the form or book a call. If anything here doesn't sit right, email me and I'll delete what I have on you within a day.

What I collect

  • What you type into the audit-request form on the home page: your name, practice name, city, email, optional website, and any notes you choose to add.
  • If you book a call through Calendly, whatever Calendly collects on my behalf: your name, email, time zone, and the date you picked.
  • Basic, non-identifying analytics about who visits the page (rough country, what page they came from, what browser they're using). I don't use any tracking that follows you to other sites.

What I do with it

  • I email you back. Usually the next morning, US time.
  • I send you the written audit. Once.
  • If you don't reply, I might send one short follow-up. After that I stop. No drip campaigns. No newsletter list you have to unsubscribe from.
  • I don't sell your information. I don't share it with anyone. There's no agency behind me to share it with.

Who else sees it

Three companies, technically:

  • Formspree. Handles the form submissions and forwards them to my inbox. Their privacy policy.
  • Calendly. Handles the call-booking flow if you use it. Their privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare. Hosts the page itself. They see what visitors to any web host see (IP, browser, requested page). Their privacy policy.

Cookies

The page itself doesn't set tracking cookies. The Calendly popup, if you open it, sets its own cookies to make the booking work. If you don't open Calendly, no cookies get set by anything other than your browser's normal session.

If you want me to delete what I have

Email arman@armanleads.com with anything that lets me find your record: your name, your practice name, or the email address you used. I'll delete the row from Formspree and any notes I have within one business day. No questions, no save call.
